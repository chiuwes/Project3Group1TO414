Affect of Holidays on Different Departments

To analyze the affect of holidays across different departments, we were able to aggregate data into the categories of departments, and our holiday dummy variable. Note that we used the mean weekly sales rather than total month sales or sum of sales. This is because for every month there are less holiday weeks than there are normal weeks. From our analysis, we found that there is a net positive impact of holidays in the big picture. Though, it seems to be high variance in the differences, with not much less than 50% of departments negatively impacted by holidays.

### Aggregating Data on by Department and Holiday dummy variable
